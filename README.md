# EcoQuest AI – Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform

## Overview

EcoQuest AI is a smart Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, sustainability challenges, and AI-powered recommendations.

The platform transforms carbon awareness into actionable daily habits by helping users identify their environmental impact, receive tailored improvement suggestions, and monitor their progress over time.

---

# Problem Statement Alignment

### Challenge Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

### How EcoQuest AI Solves This

EcoQuest AI directly addresses this challenge by:

* Helping users **understand** their environmental impact through an interactive carbon footprint assessment.
* Helping users **track** their sustainability journey using dashboards, eco scores, and challenge completion tracking.
* Helping users **reduce** their footprint through personalized recommendations, daily eco-friendly challenges, and AI-powered guidance.
* Providing simple and actionable sustainability improvements that users can realistically adopt in daily life.

# Approach and Logic

The platform follows a four-stage sustainability improvement model:

## 1. Understand

Users complete a Carbon Footprint Quiz covering:

* Transportation habits
* Food consumption patterns
* Home energy usage
* Shopping behavior
* Waste management habits

The collected data is analyzed to estimate the user's environmental impact.

---

## 2. Analyze

Based on user responses, the system generates:

* Carbon Footprint Estimate
* Eco Score
* Carbon Personality
* Impact Breakdown by Category

This helps users identify the areas contributing most to their footprint.

---

## 3. Recommend

The AI Sustainability Coach provides personalized recommendations based on:

* Quiz responses
* Carbon Personality
* Impact categories
* User behavior patterns

Recommendations focus on practical lifestyle improvements rather than generic advice.

---

## 4. Improve

The platform encourages sustainable habits through:

* Daily Challenges
* Progress Tracking
* Eco Score Improvements
* Personalized Action Plans

Users can continuously improve their sustainability performance over time.

# Assumptions Made

The following assumptions were made during development:

1. Carbon footprint calculations use simplified sustainability estimation models suitable for awareness and educational purposes.

2. User-provided information is assumed to be accurate.

3. Recommendations are designed to encourage positive environmental behavior rather than provide scientific carbon accounting.

4. Sustainability challenges are based on commonly accepted environmentally friendly practices.

5. The platform prioritizes usability and awareness over highly complex environmental calculations.
